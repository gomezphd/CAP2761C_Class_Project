About Me

Professional Background

I am a licensed psychologist and college professor with dual appointments in psychology and data science. I have extensive experience in both fields, combining my knowledge to advance research and practical applications in healthcare technology and user experience.

Education

I hold a Ph.D. in Psychology and a Master's degree in Data Science. My academic background has provided me with a solid foundation in both qualitative and quantitative research methodologies, as well as advanced data analysis techniques.

Skills

Data Analysis

Expertise in analyzing complex data sets using statistical software and programming languages such as Python and R.

Research Methodology

Proficient in designing and conducting experimental and observational studies to investigate psychological phenomena.

Healthcare Technology

Experience in developing and implementing technology solutions to improve healthcare outcomes and patient experiences.

User Experience Design

Skilled in designing user-centric interfaces and conducting usability testing to enhance product usability and accessibility.
